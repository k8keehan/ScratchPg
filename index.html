Learn to speak like a Kiwi!

  1. 'Sweet As'
    • “thank you”
    • "it's all good"
    • "no worries"
    • "that's cool"
  2. 'She'll be right'
    • "She'll be right" is classic Kiwi speak and another synonym for no worries
  3. 'Chur'
    • The meaning of chur is essentially thank you.
    • it can also mean "sweet as" or "that's awesome"
  4. 'Tu meke'
    • The literal translation from Māori is ‘too much’.
    • It's a way of expressing gratitude for generous acts
  5. 'Hard out'
    • A way of emphatically agreeing with someone
    • "Auckland has some great cafes." "Hard out!"
  6. 'Yarn'
    • Kiwis love yarns - that is, they love a friendly chat.
